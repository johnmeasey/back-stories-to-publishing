The back stories to publishing in Biological Sciences: a collection of reflections

Send me your chapters

You can send chapters directly to me at john@measey.com, or DM me on Twitter @AfriHerp. This book will be published Open Access online using Bookdown (Xie, 2016a). I will lightly edit chapters prior to posting them. If you want to make it as easy as possible, write your chapter in RMarkdown (Xie, Allaire & Grolemund, 2018), and please send any citations in BibTex format.

If the book gets interesting, we can approach a publisher, but it should always remain Open Access.
